Bolton Under 5s is managed by two committes:

A management committee

Members are elected by the parents of the children who attend the preschool.  We hold an election for new comittee members at our annual AGM.

A fundraising committee

Members organise a variety of fun events during the year to raise extra funds for the benefit of the children in preschool. 


Both committess aid the preschool through:
  • Managing the preschool's finances
  • Employing and managing the staff.
  • Making sure the preschool has, and works to, policies that help it to provide a high quality service.
  • Ensuring that the Principles of the EYFS are fully implemented
  • Making sure the preschool works in partnership with the children's parents
  • Arranging social and fundraising events.
